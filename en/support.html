Frequently asked questions

How do I import a recipe from Instagram or TikTok?

Tap + in your collection → "Import from social media" and paste the link. The app fetches the text and automatically turns it into a recipe card, including a photo from the platform.

How do I share my shopping list with my family?

Go to Settings → shared shopping list and create a family code, or enter an existing one. Everyone with the same code shares one list.

Can I add my own photo to a recipe?

Yes. Open the recipe → Edit → Choose photo, and select a photo from your library.

What's included in the subscription?

You start with 7 days free. After that, a subscription gives you unlimited importing and the AI features. Viewing and exporting your recipes always stays free. Manage or cancel your subscription via Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone.

Where are my recipes stored?

Your recipes live in your own iCloud and sync across your devices. See our privacy policy for details.
